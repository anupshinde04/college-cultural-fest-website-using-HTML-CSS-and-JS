debate

REG. FEES: INR 50
PRIZES WORTH: INR 1,000

ABOUT

Put your soft skills and convincing skills at work in this event and try to convince the opposition team that you are right.

RULES

1. It is a team event.
2. There will be two team propositions and opposition.
3. Proposition supports the motion and opposition opposes the motion.
4. Proposition always starts with the opening speech i.e. the first speaker always comes from the proposition side.
5. Time limit for all the rounds is 3 minutes .(1.5 mins for each team).
6. Event head's decision is considered final

ROUNDS

ROUND 1: The first speaker from the proposition is followed by the first speaker of opposition.
ROUND 2: Here the first question is raised by the opposition first. You can cross-question your opponents based on the points mentioned in the first round.
ROUND 3: The second speaker from the proposition is followed by the second speaker of opposition.
